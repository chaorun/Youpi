Youpi Application Features
==========================

Handy features are available out of the box. With Youpi, you can:

- Easily keep track of your data
- Tag FITS images with labels
- Prepare your tasks then save them for later processing by adding them to a shopping cart
- Define sophisticated rules to match only subsets of your Condor cluster on which you can 
  run your processing jobs
- Monitor and kill your jobs running on the cluster in realtime from the interface
- Easily access all your output results
- Get statistics and generate reports
